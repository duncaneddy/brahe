    Blue Marble Image¶

    The Blue Marble images used in Brahe's plotting functions are provided by NASA and are in the public domain. For more information, visit the NASA Blue Marble website.

    Natural Earth Data¶

    Some map data used in Brahe's plotting functions is sourced from Natural Earth, which is available under the Public Domain. For more information, visit the Natural Earth website.
